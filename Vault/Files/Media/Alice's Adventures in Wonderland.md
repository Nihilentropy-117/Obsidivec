# Alice's Adventures in Wonderland

Wonderland challenges readers to explore societal norms, engage with the absurd, and navigate contradictions and paradoxes of life. Alice's journey of continual transformation and self-discovery serves as both a cautionary tale and an invitation to embrace chaos, question beliefs, and free ourselves from restrictive rules.
